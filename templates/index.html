Deck Monsters

This game works basically like the card game 'WAR.' Each player starts with 26 monster cards and those cards face off in battle one at a time. The winner of each battle keeps both cards. Unlike cards in typical "War", moster cards gain a level whenever they win a battle. Additionally, players can choose from three moves:

Magic Strike

Deals damage between 50% and 150% of the monster's intelligence.

Attack

Deals damage equal to the monster's strength

Backstab

Has a chance, based on dexterity, of dealing 100 damage, or NONE
